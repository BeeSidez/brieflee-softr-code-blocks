Softr signup · left-side image variants

Twelve options for the image you upload into the Softr signup's left side. Every artboard is 1080 × 1080 — a safe square that crops cleanly under Softr's "Cover" fit, and works whether you keep the image full-bleed or pair it with the heading + body text.

  • Text OFF (image carries everything): V1 product, V2 testimonial, V5 bold type, V6 score card, V7 social proof, V10 stat, V12 checklist
  • Text ON (works alongside Softr's heading + body): V3 clapboard, V4 stickers, V8 brain, V11 tripod — just imagery, no copy
  • In between: V9 pass/fail uses light copy that complements but doesn't fight Softr text

To download: hover any artboard and click Download PNG in its top-right corner. The file saves at full 1080×1080 resolution at 2× pixel density (so it stays crisp on retina displays).

Background gradient on every variant matches the Softr side-section bg, so the image dissolves into it edge-to-edge with no visible seam. Drag artboards to reorder, click to focus.
